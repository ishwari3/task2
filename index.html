Cherry Timer (UI/UX design)

Cozy Pomodoro Timer is a UI/UX concept designed in Figma, inspired by a pixel-art aesthetic to create a warm and engaging study experience. The project focuses on intuitive user flow across multiple screens, including the ready state, timer settings, and active session view. I emphasized visual consistency, reusable components, and clear interaction states such as start, pause, reset, and sound toggle. This design reflects my interest in blending functionality with personality-driven interfaces.
